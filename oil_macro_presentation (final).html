CMBS — Commercial Mortgage-Backed Securities

Commercial property loans → bundled into bonds → sold to investors (pension funds, insurers, banks) → when borrowers can't pay, the delinquency rate spikes → investors holding those bonds take losses. At 12.34% office delinquency right now, this is where the next systemic credit stress is most likely to emerge — especially with $875B+ in CRE loans maturing in 2026 that need to refinance at much higher rates. — Blake Harris · Housing & Credit Risk Analysis · March 2026
